About

Learn more about me and my mission

My Mission

I believe that quality IT education should be accessible to everyone. I gather the best free courses from around the web in one place, helping you start a career in tech without financial barriers.

Why I Built This

I saw that many great courses were scattered across the internet. My goal is to bring them together in one convenient catalog so you can easily find what you need.

Community

This is a community project. Anyone can suggest courses to add. Together we create the best free IT education catalog.

Open Source

Oncorplat is free and open source. I believe education should be free. Help me make IT accessible to everyone.
